# Dext Framework: Mascot Design Guidelines

This document defines the visual identity, anatomy, and signature traits of the official Dext Framework mascot. The goal is to ensure visual consistency across all representations of the mascot, whether in high-performance banners, 2D animations, or physical plush toys.

## 1. Core Concept and Identity
The Dext mascot (inspired by the cats Nala and Lana) represents the framework's core values: **Speed, Lightweight (Zero-Allocation), Intelligence, and Reliability**. 
To harmonize the "Cyberpunk Speedster" (Turbo) aesthetic with the "Friendly/Huggable" (Cute) aesthetic, the chosen species is feline. A mix of the aerodynamic silhouette of an **Oriental Shorthair cat** with friendly and expressive traits.

### Signature Traits
These details must **NEVER** change, regardless of the illustration style:
1. **Fur Color Palette:** The body is predominantly Vibrant Blue (Dext Blue). The belly, chest, and paw tips feature a contrasting lighter blue or light tan/beige color.
2. **The Collar:** A high-tech collar. The central pendant/medal features the word **"Dext"** written in a legible, futuristic font (to ensure brand recognition).
3. **Ear Shape:** Erect and slightly pointed feline ears, designed to cut through the wind (pinned back when in motion, straight and alert when still).
4. **Confident Gaze:** The eyes never convey pure aggression, but rather absolute focus, intelligence, and a confident "smirk".

## 2. Official Variations

### A. "Turbo" Version (Performance & Tech)
Focused on illustrating the framework's absurd performance and engineering. Ideal for the README, technical presentations, and t-shirts (swag).
* **Physique:** Lean, tense muscles, and aerodynamic.
* **Movement:** Explosive sprinting pose. The paws do not touch the ground.
* **Visual Effects:** Leaves a trail of glowing blue/cyan lines simulating motherboard circuit traces (PCB traces).
* **Expression:** Absolute focus. Eyes are slightly narrowed due to speed, with a subtle, confident "smirk" of someone who masters what they do. No angry faces!

> **Generation Prompt (Turbo Version):**
> *A sleek, stylized cyber-feline mascot running at extreme high speed. The cat has a slender, aerodynamic body like an Oriental Shorthair, vibrant blue fur (color #0055FF) with light cyan (color #00D2FF) accents on the paws and underbelly. It wears a high-tech dark collar with a glowing neon orange (color #FF8C00) medal featuring the word 'Dext' written on it. The cat has pointed ears pinned back by the wind. Its expression is highly focused and confident, with eyes slightly narrowed against the wind, and a cool, subtle smirk (NOT angry, NOT aggressive, NO furrowed brows). It leaves a trail of glowing cyan circuit board lines behind it. Dark tech background (color #0B1120), vector art style, clean edges, e-sports logo aesthetic, highly detailed.*

### B. "Cute/Plush" Version (Community & Swag)
Focused on charisma and accessibility. Ideal for stickers, welcome videos, friendly error messages, and especially for designing **Physical Plush Toys**.
* **Physique:** Kitten proportions (Chibi). Slightly larger head relative to the body, large erect pointed feline ears (NOT floppy), cute small nose, large and "cushioned" paws.
* **Movement:** Sitting attentively with a friendly smile, or walking happily.
* **Visual Effects:** No intense cybernetic trails. Flatter colors and soft shadows, with a friendly sparkle in the eyes.
* **Expression:** Wide smile, large and expressive eyes. Conveys the feeling of a developer's best friend.

> **Generation Prompt (Cute Version):**
> *A cute, friendly kitten mascot of a blue cat, high-quality modern 2D animation style. The kitten has a slightly larger head, large erect pointed feline ears (NOT floppy), big expressive cat eyes, a cute small nose, and soft rounded paws. It has vibrant blue fur (color #0055FF) with light tan or cyan (color #00D2FF) paws. Around its neck is a tech collar with a glowing neon orange (color #FF8C00) medal featuring the word 'Dext' clearly written on it. The kitten is sitting happily and smiling playfully, looking very approachable and huggable. Clean vector style, white or solid light background, plush toy aesthetic.*
